# Champion Audit

Validate whether your champion is real or an imposter. 6-test framework, scored 0-60.

## When to Use
- Deal entering Negotiating stage and champion not validated
- Champion feels warm but no internal advocacy evidence
- Deal stalls unexpectedly after positive conversations

## Process

### Step 1: Load Champion Context
Read deal memory. Identify claimed champion. Pull all evidence of champion behavior.

### Step 2: Run 6-Test Framework (0-10 each, 35+ = real)

**Test 1: Motive** - Personal reason to solve this problem?
- 8-10: Articulated personal consequences in own words
- 5-7: Acknowledged problem affects their team
- 0-4: Evaluating because told to / no personal stake

**Test 2: Influence** - Can they get things done internally?
- 8-10: Moved budget, changed timelines, overridden objections
- 5-7: Has a seat at the table but hasn't flexed for us
- 0-4: Respected but lacks authority / junior

**Test 3: Access to Economic Buyer** - Can they get us to the signer?
- 8-10: Scheduled or offered meeting with EB
- 5-7: Reference EB by name and priorities
- 0-4: Don't know or won't share who approves

**Test 4: Internal Selling** - Advocating when we're not in the room?
- 8-10: Built internal docs, presented to leadership, recruited stakeholders
- 5-7: Mentioned us positively to colleagues (confirmed)
- 0-4: Attends calls but no internal advocacy evidence

**Test 5: Information Sharing** - Giving real intel?
- 8-10: Shared internal docs, org charts, budget cycles
- 5-7: Answers direct questions but doesn't volunteer
- 0-4: Vague or contradictory about internal process

**Test 6: Reciprocal Investment** - Investing time and political capital?
- 8-10: Rescheduled own meetings, brought new people, done homework
- 5-7: Shows up consistently and engages
- 0-4: Attends when convenient, cancels easily

### Step 3: Classify

| Score | Classification | Action |
|---|---|---|
| 50-60 | **Power Champion** | Give them ammunition |
| 35-49 | **Real Champion** | Coach their gaps |
| 20-34 | **Friendly Contact** | They like you but can't close. Find the real champion. |
| 0-19 | **Imposter** | Stop investing |

### Step 4: Coaching Plan
For 20-49 scores: identify weakest test, design a move to strengthen it, give champion homework.

## Output Format
```
CHAMPION AUDIT: [Name] | [Title] | [Deal]
TOTAL: [X]/60 - [Classification]

1. Motive:              [X]/10
2. Influence:           [X]/10
3. Access to EB:        [X]/10
4. Internal Selling:    [X]/10
5. Information Sharing: [X]/10
6. Reciprocal Investment: [X]/10

WEAKEST: [test name]
COACHING MOVE: [specific action]
```

## Never Do
- Never assume champion from title alone
- Never score based on friendliness. Warmth without action is an imposter signal.
- Never share audit scoring with the prospect
